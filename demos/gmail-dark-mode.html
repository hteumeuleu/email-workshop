What's A Smashing Online Workshop Like?

With online workshops, we aim to give you the same experience and access to experts as in an in-person workshop, without needing to leave your desk. So you can learn at your own pace, in your own time, and follow interactive exercises along the way.

Our workshops are all around front-end and UX, and they are structured differently, but all of them are packed with practical examples. You can also ask questions during dedicated Q&A time, and you'll get all workshop materials and recordings, too.
